What can you do?

Recycling helps to conserve natural resources such as glass, plastic, paper and oil. Reusing these materials will place less strain on our natural resources and lower the cost of production. Increases safety in the workplace and community Waste materials can be harmful to your workers, as well as the surrounding environment.

It can save your employees and visitors from illness and accidents at work. This could include anything from putting your rubbish in the bin to sorting out waste into the correct recycling containers. Adheres to CO2 targets Carbon Reduction Commitment targets can be set by local authorities, designed to reduce CO2 emissions.
